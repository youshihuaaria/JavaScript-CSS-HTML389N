Welcome to Canvas

Click and Drag anywhere in the box below to draw. You can even change the color of your brush using any hex color.


Recording Instruction

  1. Click 'Start Recording'
  2. Draw whatever you want (you can change color too)
  3. When done, click 'Stop Recording'
  4. Watch your recording by clicking 'Play'
  5. Or save it by clicking 'Save Recording'
